Elevator Pitch

Have you ever been struggling to find a 5th class with less than 100 pages of reading? Signed up for a class only to discover that you couldn’t PDF it? Or maybe you’re a BSE student with an perfectly rational fear of papers after writing sem. Fear not! You will find recourse at ReCourse (ba-dum-tss), a re-invented course search engine!

We’ve heard your complaints about the Course Offerings’ search engine. Why do I have to click back and forth between the search and results page? Why can’t we search for courses by certificate? Who even has the time to click into each course? ReCourse addresses all of these issues—and more!

Our goal is to personalize course filtering to your needs. Prefer courses with problem sets? Only want to take classes without graded participation? With ReCourse, you can search by grade breakdown, amount of reading, and course evaluation ratings. You can even enter in your current schedule to filter out courses with time conflicts.

So when it’s time for your next course selection, search on with ReCourse!
